Ash Tyndall

Ash is a Senior Software Engineer specialising in end-to-end software system design, implementation and deployment for startups, with an emphasis on Amazon Web Services DevOps, Ruby on Rails, Python, React and Natural Language-focused Machine Learning.

Ash has 13 years of professional software development experience, and has worked for a number of successful Perth startups and private clients.

Right now he splits his time between leading all aspects of Switch Batteries' cloud battery management platform, and assisting TidyHQ with cloud modernisation and feature development.

Before that, he worked with Appbot, a leading platform for app reviews & ratings analysis, where he is designed and oversaw major AWS cloud modernisation projects, built their next generation natural language insights systems and developed high-performance features used by their diverse customer base of companies like Twitter, Microsoft, BMW and Deutsche Bank.
