Ash Tyndall

Ash is a full-stack developer specialising in Natural Language-focused Machine Learning, Amazon Web Services DevOps, Ruby on Rails and React.

He is currently works with Appbot, an app-review aggregation startup, where he helps scale their AWS-based infrastructure and improve the accuracy and effectiveness of their natural language analysis.

Ash's honours thesis, CountIR, lead him to graduate top of his UWA class. His research was published in the prestigious IEEE Sensors Journal and the project was a finalist for the WAITTA INCITE Awards Best Student Project of the Year.

He also holds a Bachelor of Computer Science triple-majoring in Systems, Computation and Web Design, also from UWA.
